Week 17 is a cumulative application of everything from Chapters 1–10. You will work with three files from the Outlander's Caravan, a trading operation that runs routes between settlements in a post-collapse wasteland: a trade log, a settlements registry, and a crew roster. The assignment asks you to load and prepare the data, combine the three files into a working DataFrame, answer a set of operational questions, produce visualizations, deliver a multi-panel summary figure, and present your findings to the caravan master in a video.

## Week 17 tasks

1. Complete the **Week 17 Assignment** (Outlander's Caravan season report).
2. Record and submit your video reflection.

## Knowledge goals for Week 17

By the end of Week 17, you should be able to:

* combine three related files into a working DataFrame using merge, including a deliberate decision about how to handle rows that do not match across files
* identify and resolve data quality issues encountered during preparation, with markdown that explains each decision
* answer operational questions about a dataset using the appropriate combination of grouping, aggregation, pivot tables, and cross-tabulation
* produce visualizations that support specific findings using the Figure and Axes approach
* save a cleaned DataFrame and a multi-panel summary figure to disk
* present findings to a non-technical stakeholder, grounding each finding in a specific result from your notebook
